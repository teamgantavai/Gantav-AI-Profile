# 🛰️ Gantav AI: System Ethics & App Rules

Welcome to the future of AI-driven education. To maintain the integrity of our neural learning ecosystem, all architects and engineers must adhere to the following rules.

---

## 1. Profile Integrity
- **Authenticity**: Your profile represents your digital footprint in the AI landscape. Keep your name and career goals (Senior AI Engineer, etc.) realistic and professional.
- **Avatar Personalization**: Use the built-in Persona Randomizer or upload a high-quality gallery photo. Avatars must remain respectful and relevant.
- **Credential Verification**: Only link certificates that have been earned through Gantav or recognized partner academies (e.g., Gantav x DeepLearning.AI).

## 2. Growth & Progression
- **Streak Maintenance**: Consistent learning builds Neural Plasticity. Missing a day resets your streak; maintaining it multiplies your Gantav Points (GP).
- **Points (GP)**: Points are earned by completing modules and verifying credentials. Attempting to manipulate GP points through script exploitation will result in a level reset.
- **Evolution**: Your rank (Professional, Intermediate, Architect) is based on your total XP. Higher ranks unlock exclusive neural architectures and early-access features.

## 3. Community & Intelligence
- **Goal Intelligence**: The AI-driven goal tracker is a tool for focused growth. Respect the system's recommendations for mastering neural architectures.
- **Privacy**: We respect your neural data. Ensure your location and bio do not reveal sensitive PII (Personally Identifiable Information) beyond professional requirements.
- **Synergy**: Collaborate, don't contaminate. Share your progress to inspire others, but keep the ecosystem clean of spam.

## 4. Nexus Arena (Leaderboard)
- **Fair Competition**: Compete actively within your Cohort or Global league, but manipulation of XP to boost rankings is strictly forbidden.
- **Collaboration**: The arena encourages collaboration. Top ranks unlock special peer-mentorship ("Collab") features. Do not use this to spam other learners.
- **Privacy Controls**: If you prefer not to be ranked, you may toggle your visibility to 'Incognito' in Account Settings (coming soon).

## 5. Technical Guidelines
- **Offline Mode**: The platform works offline, but progress is only synchronized when a stable network is detected.
- **Optimized Experience**: To ensure visual stability, avoid heavy DOM manipulation. The app uses GPU-accelerated blurs and SVG-based rendering for peak performance.
- **System Theme**: Respect the dark/light mode toggle. The dark-mode interface is designed for high-focus neural sessions.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Failure to follow these rules may result in a degradation of your **Architect Status** or a temporary suspension of your developer credentials.
